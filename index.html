About Us

Your Pathway to IT Certification

At OC-TECH, we specialize in training students to become CompTIA certified technicians. Our comprehensive online programs are designed to equip you with the knowledge and practical skills needed to excel in the IT industry.

Our expert instructors bring years of real-world experience to the virtual classroom, ensuring you receive the highest quality education. We pride ourselves on our practical approach to IT training, focusing on hands-on skills that employers value.

All our training is conducted online, providing you with the flexibility to learn at your own pace while receiving the same quality instruction as in-person classes.

We offer training for all major CompTIA certifications including A+, Network+, Security+, and more. Our courses are regularly updated to reflect the latest exam objectives and industry trends.

Training Requirements

  • Stable internet connection
  • Computer or laptop
  • Microphone for interactive sessions
  • Webcam (recommended)
  • Dedication to learning
  • Basic computer literacy
  • 10-15 hours per week for study
